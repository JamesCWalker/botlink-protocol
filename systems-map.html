BOTLINK PROTOCOL

Systems Architecture Map

This document provides a high-level overview of the BOTLINK PROTOCOL subsystem architecture. It is intended for Operator Preview participants and internal ASRG personnel.

MotionGraph Engine

Role: Dynamic motion synthesis and operator-aligned movement generation.

Key Functions:

  • Predictive motion blending
  • Kinematic anchoring
  • Adaptive gait correction

PersonaLayer

Role: Behavioral modulation and interaction tone shaping.

Active Profile: NeutralHost

Attributes:

  • Warmth: 0.7
  • Responsiveness: 0.9
  • Behavioral micro-adjustments based on operator presence

SensorMesh

Role: Unified perception bus and environmental awareness layer.

Capabilities:

  • Passive environmental scanning
  • Operator recognition
  • Contextual priority routing

ReflexPack

Role: Safety-first micro-loop system for immediate corrective action.

Modes:

  • JointGuard
  • GripMonitor
  • BalanceAssist

Sandbox Autonomy

Role: Constrained autonomy envelope for micro-actions supporting operator workflow.

Notes:

  • Strict safety guarantees
  • Limited self-initiated behaviors
  • Operator comfort prioritization observed
